DELLY

DELLY is an integrated split-read and paired-end structural variant discovery and genotyping method suited for massively parallel sequencing data.

Software

Software packages I have written or contributed to.
  • DELLY is a paired-end method for structural variant calling.
  • nRex is a meta-method for somatic single-nucleotide variant calling.
  • I previously worked on SeqAn an open source library for sequence analysis algorithms, implementing graph algorithms and algorithms for multiple sequence alignment.
  • I wrote various quality control tools for short read illumina data which I recently integrated into Delly.

Projects

Projects I currently work on or have contributed in the past.
  • 1000 Genomes, a deep catalog of human genetic variation where I focused on identifying structural variants.
  • International Cancer Genome Consortium, where I primarily worked on pediatric brain tumors.
  • ICGC-TCGA DREAM Mutation Calling challenge, where I contributed calls from DELLY and nRex.
  • SeqAn, where I contributed graph algorithms and algorithms for multiple sequence alignment.
